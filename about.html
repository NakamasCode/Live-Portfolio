About Me

Hi, I’m Dedoyin Timi. I hold a degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, which gave me a solid foundation in problem-solving and technical thinking. I am also a **certified shoemaker**, running my brand JohnTimeless, where I create handmade, timeless footwear. My focus is on applying creativity and practical skills to projects that combine precision, functionality, and aesthetic appeal.
